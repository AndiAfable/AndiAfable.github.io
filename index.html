The world hasn’t been at its finest since the virus COVID-19 hit the world. The world has fallen from the small bit of grace it contained. The economy is taking a hit, hospitals and their workers are taking a hit, society’s sanity is taking a hit. Basically, everything imaginable was affected by this virus.

The important people of the world have been keeping tabs on our progress to counter the hits being thrown at us. They want us all to contribute to a solution to get us back on our feet. This is why they’ve asked us to do social distancing, wear a mask, and stay at home if possible. Less people in an area = less people that can get infected.

Pharmaceutical compapnies are scrambling to develop vaccines to help us fight this scourge.
